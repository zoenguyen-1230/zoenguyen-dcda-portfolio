Welcome

Welcome to my DCDA capstone portfolio, where I explore how culture, media, and data come together in digital spaces. Throughout the semester, this site will feature my projects, process, and experiments using both creative and analytical tools. My goal is to build a final project that blends data, storytelling, and design around topics I care about, such as travel, food, and digital culture.

Each page represents a different lab assignment, building from foundational web development skills to data visualization and interactive mapping.

About Me

Who I am: Hi! I’m Zoe Nguyen, a senior majoring in Strategic Communication with a minor in Digital Culture & Data Analytics. I’m interested in how culture, media, and data intersect in digital spaces. One fun fact about me: I love documenting travel and everyday moments through food photos, cafe hopping, and short-form digital storytelling.

My interests: I’m excited by questions around digital culture, social media, identity, and how people make meaning online. I’m especially drawn to projects involving travel, food, music, and visual storytelling, as well as how platforms shape community and representation.

What I hope to learn: From this course, I hope to strengthen my data analytics and technical skills, especially using tools like Python, data visualization, and web-based storytelling to better support creative, culture-focused projects. I want to become more confident translating ideas into data-driven digital work and building a portfolio that reflects both analytical and creative thinking.

Semester Reflection

What I learned: So far, I’ve learned how to set up a GitHub portfolio and understand the basic structure of a website using HTML and CSS.
